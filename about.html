Who are we ?

A shared vision of excellence in the ignited minds of experienced faculty member led to DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY came into existence on 20th January 2008. Driven by a shared pursuit of results, the founders of DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY are determined to be the trend setters in the field of JEE (Main + Advanced), VITEEE, BIT SAT preparation.

Faculty at DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY are from reputed Institutes of Delhi & Kota and they are highly experienced in teaching methodology, optimizing time, maximizing performance which is followed by the reputed Institutes of Delhi & Kota, which led to more than thousand selections in JEE (Main Advanced) every year.

At the outset, we would Like to emphasize that DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY is committed towards the goal of JEE (Main + Advanced). For our students, we are the dues - ex machina of JEE Main + Advanced. Commitment has a new face at DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY, we demand unwavering resilience from our students and reward intelligence in ample measure.

At DIKSHA IIT ACADEMY "Success becomes habit not Just in exams, but in life as well. A competitive environment, cutting edge knowledge pool, scientifically developed teaching methodology put us far ahead of our competitors. Highly experienced, dedicated and highly trained faculty ensure that students obtain that critical edge which gives students a clear lead In the pursuit of their goal.

Diksha IIT Academy is an Institute providing training to the students aspiring for IIT /VIT/NIT/BITSAT/State board since 2008. We have also started Foundation Program for Class 6, 7, 8, 9 & 10 students from 2012. Details of our program are given below.
